SinLan Alpha v.0.1.0 Includes - Learn! A casual, dictionary like tab where you can learn the basics of ASL. - Test! Using your camera, you can practice your ASL with the use of a hand and or body tracker depending on what letter or word(s) you are practicing. Use the "Body skeleton" switch for words such as "Mother, Father" etc. Select the word or letter you want to work on and adjust your hands or body until it is correct! -Practice! This time, the application will pick letters, numbers, or common words to quiz you! -Phrases These phrases are commonly used within the ASL community. Thanks to SignWithRobert for the free reference Gifs! -Translate This mode will test you on your knowledge of basic ASL, allowing you to learn how to read hand spelled words.
